FHIR Conversion Mapping Rules

Full-stack healthcare interoperability tool — converts HL7 v2.x messages and raw EHR pipe-delimited records to FHIR R4 using both rule-based and AI-powered engines. Supports Epic, Cerner, Meditech, Allscripts, athenahealth, eClinicalWorks, NextGen and any custom HL7 variant.

Conversion Modes

ModeDescriptionEngines Available
HL7 → FHIRConvert HL7 v2.x messages (ADT, ORM, ORU, SIU, MDM, DFT, VXU, MFN, BAR, ACK) to FHIR R4 BundleRule-based · AI (Claude / Groq)
FHIR → HL7Convert FHIR R4 Bundle back to HL7 v2.x messageRule-based
EHR → FHIRConvert raw EHR pipe-delimited records (PATIENT, ENCOUNTER, DIAGNOSIS, LAB…) to FHIR R4Rule-based · AI (Claude / Groq)
